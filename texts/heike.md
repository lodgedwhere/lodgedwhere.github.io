**From The Tale of the Heike (Heike Monogatari)**

Reflecting a theme of impermanence (無常, mujō):

*The sound of the Gion shōja bells echoes the impermanence of all things; the color of the sōla flowers reveals the truth that the prosperous must decline. 
The proud do not endure, they are like a dream on a spring night; the mighty fall at last, they are as dust before the wind.*
