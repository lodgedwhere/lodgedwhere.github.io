**The gift**

*For Maharaji, Neem Karoli Baba, who sent me Ram Dass at the beginning of my journey toward Truth:*

``` 
In both sorrow and joy I say:
I cannot give you anything.
I can only hold your hand,
and look into your eyes,
as we both let go
of the lies we tell ourselves—
until there is no *you* or *I* left
to avoid the ungraspable present moment.

“Love everyone and tell the truth”:
When you can truly do both,
they are the same movement. 

```
