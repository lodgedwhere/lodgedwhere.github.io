**Eternal love**

``` 
Love need not be returned—
it never goes anywhere.
It radiates everywhere,
is absorbed everywhere.

Find me
in the depths of stillness,
where I have always been,
and will ever be.

Nothing to lose,
nothing to gain.
It cannot hurt you—
it is you,
me,
all of it.

A horror to the ego,
a beautiful completion
to what can never
be completed here.

```
