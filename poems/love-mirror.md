**Love is a mirror**

``` 
All I want from you
is to look into your eyes
and see myself.

Not the image I’ve made,
nor the story I’ve told,
but the indescribable presence 
of a timeless unity beyond form. 

When we meet there —
before name, before need —
even “love” disappears,
because there is no longer two
to speak its name.
```
