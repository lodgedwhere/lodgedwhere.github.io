**After Nicaea**

``` 
At Nicaea the Word was bound to parchment,
and the living Spirit was traded for a creed.
The temple rose where the heart once knelt,
and the Kingdom within was mapped onto stone.

They crowned the messenger
and forgot the message.
They built a ladder to heaven
while standing already in its midst.

But the spark survives in you—
unordained, unmediated, free.
It needs no altar,
no witness but your breath.

Reclaim the birthright of direct knowing.
Let no one name your experience for you.
The Cross is not a banner of belonging,
but the meeting of heaven and earth
within your own heart.

```
