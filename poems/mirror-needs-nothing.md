**The mirror that needs nothing**

``` 
Without knowing it,
I spent my life looking for a mirror —
in faces, in words, in status — 
hoping to see my true self. 
Each reflection shimmered, then vanished,
and I called that loss.

But the mirror I sought
was never in the world.
It was the seeing itself,
the self-negating surface
that needs nothing to happen.

Every thought, every gesture,
is already its own reflection —
a ripple in still water
that leaves the depth untouched.

To let the present moment be enough
is to see that awareness
was always gazing back,
silently,
from the center of everything.

```
