**Just a poem, not a cry for help**

``` 
I got everything I ever wanted.
When I saw it would never be enough—
that the accumulation of achievement
was an unbearable burden—
the “I” collapsed.

From the pieces,
a whisper of truth assembled itself.
It spoke through my lips
and was heard with my ears,
but the voice was not mine.

Now all I want
is to meet my maker—
my true self—
and welcome Him back into my arms
in a triumphant, deathless Death 
of eternal Rebirth. 

```
