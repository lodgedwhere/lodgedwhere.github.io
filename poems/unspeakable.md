**Unspeakable**

``` 
The first stirrings
of a desire to communicate
create a world of listeners,
a universe of “you”s. 
The unity of silence
is instantly split into three:
Speaker, speech, spoken to.

From this wound, the cosmos blooms.
A Genesis 
of light, time, name, and form —
all echoing the first word
that never needed to be said:
“I”. 
```
