**I am a sinner**

``` 
The only honest statement is 
“I am a sinner.”
Not because sin is real,
but because separation is.
To say “I” is already to fall.

Yet even this confession is grace —
for when you see the error,
you are already on the other side of it.

The sinner and the saint vanish together,
like waves collapsing into still water.

Only the ocean remains,
never touched by the ripples it becomes.

```
