``` 
All doing is sheer invention—
symbols arranging themselves
into a picture
of “me.”

But:
Each meaning points
to its own absence.
Each act,
a gesture toward completion
that cannot arrive.

Still,
the symbols keep speaking,
softly,
as if ashamed to admit
they were never needed, 
never had any right
to obscure the holy silence 
that holds them softly.   
```
