**No "you" to "do"**

``` 
All doing
is a race to manifest a thought.
Letting go
cannot be *done* by you at all, 
for the thought ”let go” remains. 

See through the illusion —
doer, doing, deed —
all three arise from the void,
a single, unspeakable,
ungraspable unity.

Let go of this “you,”
and see:
it was the sole problem all along.
Not the circumstance.
Not the others.
Not even God.
```
