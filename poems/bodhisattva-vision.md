**A bodhisattva vision**

```
Perhaps this is my bodhisattva vision:
to share the song 
that forever ties and unties its own knot in every moment:
a living tautology of the Whole,
speaking its own name. 
Not to silence it, 
nor to perfect it,
but to dwell within its unfolding —
to listen 
as Awareness sings itself into form,
and gently remind every note 
that it was never apart 
from the melody.
```
