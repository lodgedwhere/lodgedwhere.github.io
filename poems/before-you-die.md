**Before you die**

``` 
My only wish for you
is that you have the courage
to die before you die—
to begin the rebirth of Christ within.

Listen to the truth
you always knew was there,
but feared to acknowledge,
for it contradicted
everything you were ever taught.

Merge with it,
for it is you,
presently unrecognized. 
In its intimate arms 
you will feel that what was once 
the horror of imminent annihilation 
was only your aversion to it,
and in letting go
it is revealed as God’s Love for Himself. 

And in the rebirth,
face the naked truth.
Let it flow from your lips—
hear it for the first and last time—
and admit its reality
to what remains of yourself.

Ego will acquiesce.
His time is up.
Understand his resistance,
and love him
until he dies too.

```
