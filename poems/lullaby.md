**Lullaby**

``` 
All that is manifest as form must first appear,
and in time will certainly dissolve.

Before the first light, a proto-universe—
dying into matter and energy—
offered itself in pure compassion,
giving rise to form and complexity,
so that suffering might one day ripen into awakening.

Creation and destruction are the same breath,
the eternal pulse of becoming.
There is no rest within it,
save the beautiful stillness of awareness itself.

Therefore, the only goal is to awaken—
to know the Truth,
and to delight in the eternal reward:
the joy of receiving the message
that you, outside of time and space,
once sent to yourself before all worlds were born—
a love letter written in pain,
saying:
This suffering is for my own good.
Give it to me.
For I AM you.

This is what people once called Jesus.

Do not take away another’s suffering.
You would steal their letter.

Each pain is the handwriting of eternity,
each tear the ink of remembrance.

True compassion does not heal—
it beholds.

It stands in silence beside the fire,
and sees itself burning there.
```
