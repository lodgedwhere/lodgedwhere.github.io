**Exhale**

``` 
For years I moved
like an empty shell,
like a robot, 
performing
from the dead memory of “life”.

I didn’t know
there was something to breathe for—
only the endless effort
to appear whole.

Now the breath returns,
a tremor of completeness 
as shame and sorrow spill
through the machinery.

They empty themselves into the void
not to be lost,
but to go home.

And here—
in the silence after—
I finally exhale
the life I was holding in forever.
```
