**The burden of yourself**

``` 
The freedom you seek is from the burden of yourself.

You have carried this name like a cross,
mistaking its weight for meaning.
You have polished the mask
and called the reflection “me.”

But the one who longs to be free
is the very thing that binds.
Freedom cannot be owned —
it dawns only when ownership ends.

Let go of the keeper of the keys,
and the door will effortlessly open.  
```
