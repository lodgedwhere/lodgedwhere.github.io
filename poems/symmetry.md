**Symmetry**

``` 
A sphere,
perfectly symmetric,
drew its own equator
and broke itself in two.

Believing it was one half,
it sought to devour the other—
to know itself.

But as the latitude rose,
so did the certainty
that the task could not be completed.

It shrank from this realization
and descended to the pole—
the tiniest flaw:
a sphere punctured by its own eye.

Nowhere to go,
but to dissolve into what is—
forgetting,
in order to remember.

```
