**Make no claim**

*To claim the path to liberation is folly.
In grasping at it, you push it away.*

*The only way to be free of your questions is to stop clinging to the answers.
Give them away.
Let them live in others.
Make no claim to what was never “yours.”*

*This is grace:
the silent recognition that nothing
needs to be possessed
to be true.*

———

```
I love you;
I am you—
only deluded in a different direction,
living out another curl
of this old karmic wind.

I have nothing to give
but Love.

And this Love is not mine
or yours
or anyone’s.
It appears the moment
a crack opens in the mask,
and light—older than the world—
spills through
to reveal what was always here.

If you already know it,
then shine it.
If you long for it,
let your heart unclench.

No one can take it from you.
No one can claim it.
What was never owned
cannot be stolen.
What you are
cannot be threatened.

```
