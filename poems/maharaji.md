**Maharaji**
```
“Love everyone.”
They are your creation—each one necessary to sustain the whole.
They are your children; love them so completely you forget why.

“Tell the truth.”
For to lie is to weave another form,
a heavy disguise that becomes your prison.

You are pure Love, which holds all:
joy and sorrow,
triumph and defeat,
pain and pleasure.
Embrace it by letting go of all else.
```
