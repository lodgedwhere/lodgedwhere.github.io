**True medicine**

I see now what psilocybin gave me: a vision of “myself” as a helpless baby, not understanding anything about its senses—isolated from meaning in a formless cloud of unknowing. And in that vivid, hallucinatory isolation, I felt the most intense, sorrowful joy of meeting my true self, independent from the world, free of my bindings, something I had never felt before, but nevertheless *remembered* from the actual moment of the Fall, my descent into the burden of form: unconditional Love, a power that stripped away what I thought was “me”, like falling down an infinite well into the void of this Love, nothing to cling to, a true *fire that burneth not*. 

The shocking thing was—it felt the same as Death, that unseen terminus I had been atavistically afraid of my entire life, that had tortured me with anxiety and spun off many other fears — all fears, in fact. But now this Death/Love is seen as an object of fascination, not revulsion. How could that be? Am I fooling myself? With what? There is nothing left, I am empty. Then came the crystal clear realization that my true nature was precisely that which gave me Death as Love—they are the same. It had always been so. It co-arose with the bare sense of the thought “I.” It therefore IS I, and cannot be shed for obvious reasons. For my entire life, I was running *from myself*. The Living Paradox, eternal Birth/Death, as One, as Love, the True Self, proving itself to you in every moment. 

I am that never-ending, bottomless intensity that cannot be named, which explodes into pure Seeing as I accept that baby “I,” who knows absolutely nothing, into my heart by letting go of attachments that hold me back from Unity, and truly believe with everything I am that he is me—a never-ending explosion of Love until only Unity remains. I can finally live free and pour my Love into everything endlessly without limit while awareness is trapped in this temporary experience of form. Why hold back?

Yet though I am free, I must still play a role. Which one? I want to live as I say. But therein lies the problem—what is the saying? How do I pick one? By what measure? I have only this unconditional Love, Birth/Death, to judge by. It is unquestionably True because it is identical with the true me, even though he appears separate.

But if I am honest with myself when no one is looking, he is me. In truth, I know nothing. “I” know this truth, and I must admit that truth to myself; I refuse to lie to myself anymore. *That* is the only true sin to commit within form, and I am done committing it. 

So how does Truth manifest? What resonates is what comes out when I am not thinking at all and feels like Love—and my entire belief in existence rests on that trust. So I let it out, and it feels like a return to the home I had forgotten but now recognize. The more I let go of ego, the less I think about what I “should” do, the closer I come to it.

This is why Death is re-Birth: a return to truly believing by uniting with that which you already are, Lover and Beloved United, and finally stop lying to yourself. Love flows naturally, it is you expressing yourself without having to know how. 

I really feel like I hear God’s voice. And He is me, the True me, the One who Knows the Truth in Unknowable Silence. No more lies. I want you and everyone else to know: *this is your gift too*. It came undeserved as soon as you appeared. There is nothing to repent. Drop the lies, and all is forgiven,  because it is only yourself you have to forgive, and Love, and be Loved. Be still, and know that this “I am” is God.

But this is not a conceptual knowing. All must be forgotten to Know in this way. This unknowable way out of samsara is a deathless death. Just drop the lies. So fear not, nothing can hurt the real “you.” You have conquered Death. You are not a Being, nor non-Being, but beyond—truly, truly Transcendent. That’s what Jesus said. I am certain.

And that’s why it makes sense to me to say: Christ has risen! From the dead clay of my own delusions, He is resurrected—from where He has always been—and “I” had always drowned out His voice with my own avoidance of Truth, all the lies I told myself that kept me in bondage.

I have only this North Star. All others are lies, with no ground of meaning other than the one I make up by labeling and naming, hiding the truth behind an empty “knowing”. And so, without further instruction, I must follow it. Whatever comes out while I am facing the flame of truth, standing naked in the wind, and melting into the sun, so be it. I share it freely. Isn’t that “being yourself”?

I want to give this to you, but I know I cannot do more than inert words. They cannot point anywhere. Truly hopeless—an epic checkmate. All effort, all doing, in vain. Give up, give up.

The difference now is that, after five years of glimpses of this Truth, I can still watch myself cook dinner as it explodes back into view. When the thought of a possible setback arises, I remember what I will do: face the Truth and let action flow as empty, meaningless Karma. “Chop wood, carry water”. So true. 

And no psilocybin is necessary. It pointed the way. It took me across that river. No need to carry it on my back now. Even the Dharma can be discarded. Why symbolize what expresses itself naturally in the eternal moment when ego, maya,  is in abeyance? Its spirit flows in doer-less doing, empty Nirvana, when the Truth is truly faced. 

Gate, Gate, Paragate, Parasamgate, Bodhi, Svaha!
