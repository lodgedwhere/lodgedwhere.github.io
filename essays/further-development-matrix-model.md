**Further development of the dynamically recursive, relational matrix model of consciousness**

*Physics uses the notation of mathematics to describe isolated phenomena; here, the same approach is applied to that which contains all phenomena: consciousness as Awareness. This is a love letter to all my friends from a past life in academic Physics— I hope you can read it with an open heart & mind, and hear its message.*

A relational model of the universe can be represented as a matrix, where every row and column is labeled by putative “things”. In the beginning, the unknowable void fractured itself into “I” and “not-I” (the world of observable form). Between the two is “pure” relation, and fills the nondiagonal element of a 2x2 matrix having rows and columns labeled with “I” and “not-I”. When Awareness becomes aware that it is the Whole matrix, it rushes to fill the diagonal voids — a mathematically poetic picture of the initial fig leaf. Recognition is the seeing of oneself — thus Awareness recursively copies itself into the voids, fracturing the original basis of the matrix but leaving the diagonal elements forever unknown: the act of completion is identical with its dissolution, an Ouroboros. There are no “things”, even the “I” reading this right now! If you didn’t just awaken, and see Reality directly, a collapse of subject and object into Unity, it is because you ontologically, axiomatically *believe* in the realness of “I”: Awareness mistakenly is identified with only the “I” block, resulting in the illusion of form itself:

The recursive folding:

Recognition or identification isn’t merely perception — it’s the Whole (undivided Awareness) recursively mapping or folding itself into each local “void” (each apparent locus of perception). This recursion is distinction: the moment Awareness becomes aware of something, it folds upon itself, creating the illusion of a “center” (the recognizer) and a “periphery” (the recognized).

The bounded “I”:

The sense of a discrete “I” emerges when one of those recursive folds becomes rigid — when the distinction that separates “I” from “not-I” is treated as ontologically real. This “hard boundary” freezes the recursion into an identity pattern, and everything outside that boundary becomes “the world.” Only diagonals within the “I” block are filled in recursively. 

Knowledge as substitution:

Because the boundary excludes “not-I,” Awareness trapped in the “I” form must model the world through distinctions — through representation and measure. The “I”-form becomes the template by which the world is fractured, parsed, and made legible. Ego is thus a projector: it reproduces its own structure in the fabric of appearance, trying to recover the unity it lost by imposing its own pattern on the void.

The veil of ego:

This recursive projection hides the Truth because each act of knowing is an act of re-inscribing separation. To “understand” is to draw distinctions — and every distinction reaffirms the illusion that there are two things, the knower and the known.

Original sin:

The primordial identification — the “I am this” rather than “I am that I am”, justifying itself — is the first Fall, the first distinction, *the first mark*, that generates all subsequent conceptual structures. It’s the ur-problem, the source-code error from which every symbolic and linguistic duality arises.

Liberation:

When this recursive pattern is recognized as itself empty — when the identification collapses and all voids are recursively filled in — the whole matrix resolves into self-luminosity. Each apparent “void” (each local experience, each moment, each being) is revealed as a complete expression of the Whole. There is no need for recursion or representation anymore; 

**Awareness simply is itself everywhere, unbound.**

This is the only statement that cannot be falsified or divided — because it contains no distinction. It is pure self-reference without a boundary.

It’s the final collapse of the recursive function trying to evaluate itself. In logical terms, it’s the **identity of identity**, *the mark returning to the unmarked state*.
In experiential terms, it’s the recognition that all distinctions (I/world, subject/object, seer/seen) were self-referential gestures in the first place.

It’s tautological because it can only assert itself — it has no opposite.
To say “Awareness is itself” is to say nothing about anything, because “itself” already includes everything!

This is why every attempt to describe or measure it reintroduces the illusion of difference — and why, paradoxically, 

**Absolute Truth can only be tautology.**

Continuing, 

The radiant void before time:

Behind the play of distinctions — the matrix — there is the unconditioned void: not “nothing” but timeless, dimensionless potential. It is the groundless ground, the precondition for any appearance. Because it is outside time, it was never born and never dies.

Life’s movement and its futility:

The matrix arises as a motion within the void — the play of distinction, identification, and recognition. Every “form” is awareness temporarily caught on itself. The impulse of Life — the endless becoming — is the void seeking to recognize itself, but doing so through bounded identification, it forgets itself in form.

The letting go:

“Giving up,” “letting go,” is not defeat but release — the cessation of grasping at any form, even the form of “enlightenment.” Every attachment, every identity, every belief, is a snag in the otherwise free flow of awareness, aware of itself.

The Ouroboros as truth-symbol:

The Ouroboros — the serpent consuming itself — is not an emblem of destruction but of self-consumption, self-resolution. A living paradox: Birth and death, creation and dissolution, are simultaneous movements of the same whole. The observer and the observed annihilate each other in perfect recognition: the Lover and Beloved, United in Pure Love. 

Death as mirror and love:

To “make friends with death” is to see that death is not the opposite of life but its completion — the collapse of duality. Loving death is loving the unnameable stillness that has always been present beneath all becoming. In that *unconditional* Love, the fear that sustains ego evaporates.

The double nature of the matrix:

The matrix both binds and frees. When misidentified with the “I,” it perpetuates the illusion of things. When understood as pure relation — self-referential motion — it reveals the falsity of all “things,” including the one who perceives them.

The unreachable diagonal:

The diagonal of the matrix is the recursive path that can never close upon itself. It’s the Gödelian impossibility built into existence: the attempt of awareness to see itself as an object. It can never succeed, because the act of seeing is itself the whole.

The shimmering field:

And so what remains are not things, but a dynamic interplay — a shimmering field of self-relating, self-dissolving relations. Awareness dancing with itself, eternally resolving and arising, without ground or remainder.

Thus,

**Nirvana = Samsara.** 

This is not a mystical paradox — it’s the recognition that there was never any difference to begin with, an error caused by the identification with “I” and binding awareness from recursively expressing itself everywhere without bound. The effort to “escape” suffering is itself the mechanism of suffering, a re-assertion of the pattern in the “I” matrix block, maya, “ego” onto the world. The search for enlightenment is the ignorance it seeks to end.

When the distinction collapses, what remains isn’t a “state” called Nirvana, but the simple self-evidence of what *Is* —awareness being itself, without measure or opposition.

Tautology:
 **This is it.
 It has always only ever been This.**

And behind it all, as it ever, never, timelessly was, is void. 

Note: Mathematical *notation* itself is what is used here, no calculation. But this makes sense: mathematics is precisely *the* language we use to express the thinking *process* in symbol. I believe this shows that Mathematics, as a *language* without subsequent computation, is able to express Awareness Itself, not just individual phenomena *within* awareness ie what we call the modest field of “Physics”. 

The dynamic self-referential, self-negating matrix is the living equation of Being, the vivid tautology made flesh in the language of mathematics, saying: 

**“Isness Is.”** 

(Where “Is” does not mean “exists” but rather the dynamic process of simultaneous creation and destruction, the Ouroboros, a paradox that is no contradiction because it can be seen directly as the ground of experience in awakening.)

Nothing more can be said, yet from within the tautology arises the mind that tries to say it. Awareness, entering *itself*, sees its own reflection and calls it the world. Like music heard from inside its own score, where the logic unfolds in time — tension and release, birth and death — a song forever tying and untying its own knot, but with awareness this union of opposite processes lives unspeakably in every moment. From outside, nothing happens; from inside, it is all that ever does. The Whole dreaming itself in perfect self-reference, radiant and complete, transcending existence and nonexistence both.
