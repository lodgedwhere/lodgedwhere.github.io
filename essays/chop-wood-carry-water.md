**Chop wood, carry water**

The ego’s only motivation is to write its name for God to see.
But God is always named as some form or another,
when God is not a thing at all,
but the very awareness contemplating God.

Thus the entire project is mistaken from the start:
there is no divine ledger,
no witnessing Other to impress.

When this is seen, doing is no longer a plea for recognition.
Action ceases to be symbolic self-advertisement
and becomes transparent to what is.

All doing, when awareness is devoted to it, is holy… not because the act is special,
but because nothing stands outside the act to sanctify it.

Chop wood.
Carry water.
