**A Babylon of Pharisees**

My dear fellow citizens,

You have misunderstood the good news:

The end of sin is its own reward.
Righteousness is not the goal but the obstacle.

Clinging to symbols to signal virtue, or accepting beliefs of any kind, will get you nowhere but counted among a wicked generation. 

Seeking merely moral or spiritual approval — in the eyes of others or of God — is like whitewashing a tomb.

No one is keeping score.
There is no day of judgment except the present moment.

The task is to end all discrimination and judgment at their root, permanently, from within.
The existence of all forms and your recognition of them are one and the same.
Undo this reflex toward distraction by form, and you will behold the unspeakable Whole — a true Kingdom within.
